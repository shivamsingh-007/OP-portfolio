# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and tech leads evaluating Shivam Singh for AI engineering roles. They arrive with limited time, scanning for signal: does this person build real things? The portfolio must answer that in under 10 seconds.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio showcasing Shivam Singh's AI engineering work — autonomous agents, multi-agent systems, and full-stack projects. The site itself is proof of craft: if the portfolio is well-built, the candidate is well-built.

## Brand Personality

Dark, cinematic, intense. Spider-Man comic book aesthetic translated into web design: halftone textures, dramatic lighting, bold red accents against deep blacks. The site should feel like opening a comic panel — kinetic, confident, slightly dangerous.

## Anti-references

- Generic Bootstrap/Material template portfolios with card grids and blue gradients
- Corporate SaaS landing pages with stock illustrations and sterile whitespace
- Anything that says "modern" but means "safe" — rounded corners on everything, pastel palettes, friendly illustrations

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** — Every section demonstrates capability, not just describes it
2. **Comic book language** — Visual hierarchy through panel layouts, halftone textures, bold typography
3. **Earned attention** — Interactions reward exploration; nothing is decorative without purpose
4. **Darkness is depth** — Black backgrounds create focus and drama, not emptiness
5. **Motion with meaning** — Animations reference Spider-Man's physicality: falling, swinging, web lines

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected throughout
- Keyboard navigable
- Sufficient contrast on text (4.5:1 minimum against dark backgrounds)
- Screen reader friendly with semantic HTML and ARIA labels
